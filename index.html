SERVICES OFFERED

REFACTORIES INSTALATION AND SUPERVISION

The major activities of AHL Refactories & Services is installation and supervision of refactories. Well Qualified and experienced professional persons work as erection engineers and supervisors to carry out the given job as perscibed specification.

We provide complete turn-key,project management for your refactory installation. This means total, one-source responsibility for all aspect of the job:Demolition of existing lining, Surtface preparation,lining design, anchor selection, Anchore system welding, Vibration cast lining, rammed lining, Precasted shapes, Ceramic fiber lining (Blanket & Modules), Project scheduling, crew sizing, eqipment materials coordination and most importantly complete technical supervision in the field.

All the tools and measuring instruments used in the erection work to ensure the meticulous execution of a job are modern. A well documented Quality Control System is made available to all the engineers and supervisors at the job site.

The strength of our team lies in the execution of the jobs within the time schedule and as per the norms of Quality Control. The job planning gets maximum attention by each amd every team member. Every stage of erection work is subjected to a quality audit. The site in-charge of the project is assigned the overall responsibility for the quality and progress of the job.

INSPECTOIN AND TESTING REFACTORIES

The second most valuable services AHL Refactories & Services offer to our customers are inspection and testing of rafactories which is undertaken by us to our customers. This task is performed by the qualified, seasoned and discerning professionals associated with our company.

The team of inspectors sre well-versed with traditional and modern equipments and testing methods. The inspection and testing of refactories are carried out at the supplier's premises, as per international test procedures or as per specification laid down by the clients. After testing the refactories the appraisal documents in a standard format are submitted to the client. The client is also helped in assessing the interpretation of the test results.

Third Party Inspection for any kind of installation jobs is also carried out by us to ensure the correct method and quality of the lining by appointing an experienced Quality Control Engineer at site. A set of quality documents will be maintained for each working area and submitted to the clients at the end of the project.

MAINTENTANCE SERVICES

AHL Refactories and Services provides the services of undertaking the daily maintenance work of refactory and its related equipments in an industry at contract basis. Induction furnace lining, chipping, removing and relining can be done and it will be maintainted for uninterrupted run. Also the related equipments like Ladles,converters,hoppers and other can be relined and maintained on daily basis.

A crew headed with supervisor will be assigned to carry out al the daily basis jobs in an industry. Based on customer reques,additional man power will also be supplied during emergency needs.
